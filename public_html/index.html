Synthesism

A synthwave themed split screen racing game

Power Ups

Synthesism features 3 power ups, taking inspiration from Mario Kart. Grab a speed boost to catch up to your opponents, an oil spill to leave a trap for them or a spike bomb to make your opponent have a bad day.

group

Split Screen

Synthesism can be played with 2 people! There is a multiplayer option where you can race against your friend and the AI.

settings

Artificial Intelligence

Synthesism makes use of Artificial Intelligence techniques like raycast detection and rubber banding to ensure the cars you race against remain competitive and fair.
