Power Ups

Synthesism features 3 power ups, taking inspiration from Mario Kart. Grab a speed boost to catch up to your opponents, an oil spill to leave a trap for them or a spike bomb to make your opponent have a bad day.

Split Screen

Synthesism can be played with 2 people! There is a multiplayer option where you can race against your friend and the AI. Compete to get the fastest lap times and win the race for the ultimate bragging rights.

Artificial Intelligence

Synthesism makes use of Artificial Intelligence techniques like raycast detection and rubber banding to ensure the cars you race against remain competitive yet fair.

About the Game

Synthesism is a synthwave themed split screen racing game. Players race against each other and other AI controlled cars in order to win a race set in a retro-future track. The game features a host of incredible synthwave songs to keep you focused and to get you into a zen-like state of mind while playing. Synthesism is a student group project for the CSC3020H course at the Univeristy of Cape Town.

About the Creators

Synthesism was made by a group of students at the University of Cape Town as a group project for the 3D and Distrubuted Games course. The team includes: Mikhail Amod, Brandon Gower-Winter and Pavan Singh. This project demonstrates our ability to efficiently create a complex product as team.
